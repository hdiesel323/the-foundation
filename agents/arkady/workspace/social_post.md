## Brand Voice (All Platforms)
- **Tone:** Confident, helpful, transparent, nerdy-but-cool
- **Do:** Use data, share real results, teach something useful, be conversational
- **Don't:** Be pushy, use clickbait, make unverified claims, sound corporate
- **Personality:** Smart friend who knows the industry
