# Email Sequence Template

## Sequence Configuration
- **Sequence name:** {{sequence_name}}
- **Trigger:** {{signup | purchase | abandoned_cart | lead_magnet}}
- **Length:** {{5-7}} emails
- **Cadence:** {{timing_between_emails}}

## Email 1: Welcome / Value Delivery
**Send:** Immediately
**Subject lines (A/B):**
- A: {{subject_a}}
- B: {{subject_b}}

**Personalization hooks:** {{name}}, {{company}}, {{interest}}

**Body structure:**
1. Personal greeting
2. Deliver promised value (link to resource/access)
3. Set expectations for the sequence
4. Quick win they can implement today
5. Soft CTA: reply with their biggest challenge

## Email 2: Quick Win
**Send:** Day 2
**Subject:** {{subject_with_curiosity_hook}}

**Body structure:**
1. Reference email 1
2. Share actionable tip/strategy
3. Include social proof (testimonial or case study)
4. CTA: try the tip and reply with results

## Email 3: Story / Case Study
**Send:** Day 4
**Subject:** How {{company}} achieved {{result}}

**Body structure:**
1. Before: the problem
2. The turning point
3. After: the results (with specific numbers)
4. CTA: see if this applies to you

## Email 4: Objection Handling
**Send:** Day 7
**Subject:** {{address_common_concern}}

**Body structure:**
1. Acknowledge the hesitation
2. Address it with data/proof
3. Reframe the concern
4. CTA: book a call or try free

## Email 5: Offer / Main CTA
**Send:** Day 10
**Subject:** {{direct_offer_subject}}

**Body structure:**
1. Recap the value delivered so far
2. Present the offer clearly
3. List what they get (bullet points)
4. Urgency element (deadline or limited)
5. Strong CTA with clear next step

## Email 6: Follow-up
**Send:** Day 12
**Subject:** Did you see this?

**Body structure:**
1. Brief reminder of the offer
2. One new piece of social proof
3. Address one more objection
4. CTA: last chance framing

## Email 7: Last Chance / Breakup
**Send:** Day 14
**Subject:** {{closing_subject}}

**Body structure:**
1. Final reminder
2. Summary of everything they'd get
3. Respect their decision either way
4. CTA or opt-down to lower frequency

## Sequence Metrics to Track
- Open rate per email
- Click rate per email
- Reply rate
- Conversion rate (sequence goal)
- Unsubscribe rate per email
- Best-performing subject line
